## ğŸ’¡ Beginner Tips for Success

### 1. Start Simple
Begin with basic recipes like **air fryer bacon** or **air fryer broccoli** to learn your machine.

### 2. Preheat Your Air Fryer
Always preheat for 3 minutes at the cooking temperature for best results.

### 3. Don't Overcrowd
Place food in a single layer for even cooking.

### 4. Check Halfway Through
Shake the basket or flip food halfway through cooking time.

### 5. Use a Thermometer
For meat recipes, use a meat thermometer to ensure proper doneness.
