# Air Fryer Meal Prep Recipes

Transform your week with air fryer meal prep! Cook once, eat healthy all week long. Perfect for busy professionals, students, and families.

## Why Air Fryer is Perfect for Meal Prep

1. **Fast Cooking**: Prep multiple meals quickly
2. **No Supervision**: Set it and prep other things
3. **Easy Cleanup**: Minimal dishes to wash
4. **Consistent Results**: Same quality every time
5. **Reheats Perfectly**: Crispy texture maintained

## Essential Meal Prep Equipment

### Must-Haves
- **Glass Containers** (microwave & dishwasher safe)
- **Measuring Cups** (portion control)
- **Labels & Markers** (track dates)
- **Meal Prep Bags** (for freezing)
- **Kitchen Scale** (accurate portions)

### Nice-to-Haves
- **Bento Boxes** (separated compartments)
- **Mason Jars** (salads and overnight oats)
- **Silicone Dividers** (organize containers)

## Meal Prep Sunday Strategy

### 2-Hour Meal Prep Plan

**9:00 AM - 9:30 AM**: Plan & Prep
- Choose recipes
- Make grocery list
- Gather containers

**9:30 AM - 10:30 AM**: Shopping
- Buy ingredients
- Focus on sales

**10:30 AM - 11:00 AM**: Wash & Chop
- Prep all vegetables
- Season proteins
- Organize by recipe

**11:00 AM - 12:30 PM**: Cook
- Batch cook in air fryer
- Rotate items
- Multi-task efficiently

**12:30 PM - 1:00 PM**: Portioning
- Divide into containers
- Label everything
- Store properly
