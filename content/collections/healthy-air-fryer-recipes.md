## üí° Tips for Maximum Nutrition in Air Fryer Recipes

### 1. Choose Whole Foods
- Fresh vegetables over frozen when possible
- Lean proteins like chicken breast and fish
- Whole grains for fiber and nutrients

### 2. Minimize Oil Usage
- Use cooking spray instead of pouring oil
- Brush oil lightly for even coverage
- Choose heart-healthy oils like olive oil

### 3. Preserve Nutrients
- Don't overcook vegetables
- Use shorter cooking times
- Keep skins on when possible (like potatoes)

### 4. Add Superfoods
- Sprinkle chia seeds on finished dishes
- Add nuts for healthy fats
- Include fresh herbs for antioxidants
