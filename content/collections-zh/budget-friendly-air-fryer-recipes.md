# Budget-Friendly Air Fryer Recipes

Eating well doesn't have to be expensive! These budget-friendly air fryer recipes use affordable ingredients to create delicious meals.

## Why Air Fryers Save Money

1. **Uses Less Energy**: Up to 50% less than conventional ovens
2. **Minimal Oil**: Save on cooking oil costs
3. **No Waste**: Cooks small portions perfectly
4. **Meal Prep Friendly**: Cook in batches, save time
5. **Cheaper Cuts**: Makes budget cuts taste amazing

## Bulk Cooking Tips

### Best Foods to Cook in Bulk

1. **Chicken Drumsticks** (20-30 at once)
2. **Meatballs** (Make 50-100)
3. **French Fries** (Cut 5-10 pounds)
4. **Roasted Vegetables** (Multiple trays)
5. **Egg Rolls** (Freeze 40-50)

### Storage & Reheating

**Refrigerator** (3-4 days):
- Individual containers
- Portion sizes ready to grab

**Freezer** (3 months):
- Label with date
- Freeze flat in bags (space-saving)
- Reheat in air fryer 5-7 minutes
