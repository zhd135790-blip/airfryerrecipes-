ingredients:
  - 2 large sweet onions
  - 1 cup all-purpose flour
  - 2 eggs, beaten
  - 1 cup milk
  - 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  - Cooking spray
instructions:
  - Cut onions into 1/2-inch thick slices and separate into rings
  - Mix eggs and milk in one bowl
  - Mix panko, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and cayenne in another bowl
  - Place flour in third bowl
  - Coat each onion ring in flour, then egg mixture, then breadcrumb mixture
  - Press breadcrumbs firmly onto rings
  - Place on plate and refrigerate for 15 minutes
  - Preheat air fryer to 375°F (190°C)
  - Spray air fryer basket with cooking spray
  - Arrange onion rings in single layer
  - Spray tops generously with cooking spray
  - Cook for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden brown
  - Serve immediately with dipping sauce

## Tips for Perfect Onion Rings

- **Use sweet onions**: Vidalia or Walla Walla work best
- **Separate carefully**: Keep rings intact
- **Chill before cooking**: Helps coating stick
- **Don't overlap**: Ensures even crisping
- **Serve immediately**: Best when hot and fresh

## Coating Variations

**Extra Crispy:**
- Add cornflakes to breadcrumbs

**Beer Battered:**
- Replace milk with beer

**Gluten-Free:**
- Use almond flour and GF breadcrumbs

**Spicy:**
- Add more cayenne and chili powder
