ingredients:
  - 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  - 3 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  - 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese (optional)
instructions:
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (200°C) for 3 minutes
  - In a large bowl, toss cauliflower florets with olive oil and all seasonings
  - Place cauliflower in air fryer basket in a single layer
  - Cook for 12-15 minutes, shaking basket every 5 minutes
  - Check for desired tenderness and golden color
  - Sprinkle with parmesan cheese if desired
  - Serve immediately while hot and crispy

## Tips for Perfect Air Fryer Cauliflower

- **Cut evenly**: Similar-sized florets cook more evenly
- **Shake regularly**: Prevents burning and ensures even cooking
- **Don't overcrowd**: Single layer for best results

## Variations

- **Buffalo Style**: Toss with buffalo sauce after cooking
- **Cheesy**: Add your favorite cheese blend
- **Herbed**: Add fresh herbs like thyme, rosemary, or oregano

## Serving Ideas

- As a side dish with any protein
- Over rice or pasta
- In grain bowls
- As a healthy snack

## Storage

Cooked cauliflower keeps in the fridge for up to 4 days. Reheat in air fryer at 350°F for 5-7 minutes.
