ingredients:
  - 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and patted dry
  - 1 small onion, roughly chopped
  - 4 cloves garlic
  - 1 cup fresh parsley
  - 1 cup fresh cilantro
  - 1 teaspoon cumin
  - 1 teaspoon coriander
  - 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  - 1 teaspoon baking powder
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - Cooking spray
instructions:
  - In food processor, add chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, and cilantro
  - Pulse until mixture is coarse, not pureed
  - Add cumin, coriander, cayenne, salt, pepper, flour, and baking powder
  - Pulse until combined
  - Transfer to bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes
  - Form mixture into 16 small patties or balls
  - Brush each with olive oil
  - Preheat air fryer to 375°F (190°C)
  - Spray air fryer basket with cooking spray
  - Place falafel in single layer, not touching
  - Cook for 12-15 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy
  - Serve warm in pita with tahini sauce and vegetables

## Perfect Falafel Tips

- **Don't over-process**: Mixture should be coarse, not smooth
- **Chill the mixture**: Makes forming patties easier
- **Pat chickpeas dry**: Removes excess moisture
- **Brush with oil**: Ensures golden exterior
- **Don't skip herbs**: Fresh herbs are essential for flavor

## Serving Suggestions

**Classic Falafel Wrap:**
- Warm pita bread
- Tahini sauce
- Lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers
- Pickled turnips
- Hot sauce

**Falafel Bowl:**
- Quinoa or rice
- Hummus
- Tabbouleh
- Greek salad
- Tzatziki sauce

**Falafel Salad:**
- Mixed greens
- Cherry tomatoes
- Cucumber
- Feta cheese
- Lemon vinaigrette

## Make Tahini Sauce

Mix together:
- 1/2 cup tahini
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Water to thin
- Salt to taste

## Storage & Reheating

**Refrigerate:** Up to 5 days in airtight container
**Freeze:** Up to 3 months
**Reheat:** Air fryer at 350°F for 5-7 minutes
