ingredients:
  - 1.5 lbs white fish fillets (cod, tilapia, or mahi-mahi)
  - 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  - 1/2 cup flour
  - 2 eggs, beaten
  - 1 teaspoon cumin
  - 1 teaspoon chili powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Cooking spray
  - 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  - 2 cups shredded cabbage
  - 1 cup diced tomatoes
  - 1 avocado, sliced
  - Fresh cilantro
  - Lime wedges
  - Lime Crema (1/2 cup sour cream, juice of 1 lime, pinch of salt)
instructions:
  - Cut fish into 3-inch strips and pat dry
  - Set up breading station with flour, beaten eggs, and panko mixed with spices
  - Season fish with salt and pepper
  - Coat fish strips in flour, then egg, then panko mixture
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C)
  - Spray air fryer basket with cooking spray
  - Place breaded fish in single layer, spray tops with cooking spray
  - Cook for 8-10 minutes until golden and fish flakes easily
  - While fish cooks, mix sour cream, lime juice, and salt for crema
  - Warm tortillas in microwave for 30 seconds
  - Assemble tacos with fish, cabbage, tomatoes, avocado, cilantro, and lime crema
  - Serve with lime wedges

## Perfect Fish Taco Tips

- **Choose the right fish**: Firm white fish works best - cod, tilapia, or mahi-mahi
- **Crispy coating**: Don't skip the spray on top for extra crispiness
- **Fresh toppings**: Make tacos right after cooking fish for best texture
- **Warm tortillas**: Always warm tortillas for better flavor and flexibility
