ingredients:
  - 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  - 1/3 cup melted butter
  - 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  - 1 egg, beaten
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 1 teaspoon baking soda
  - Pinch of salt
  - 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  - 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  - 1/2 cup chocolate chips or walnuts (optional)
instructions:
  - Mash ripe bananas in large bowl
  - Stir in melted butter
  - Mix in sugar, egg, and vanilla extract
  - Sprinkle baking soda and salt over mixture and stir
  - Add flour and cinnamon, mix until just combined
  - Fold in chocolate chips or walnuts if using
  - Grease mini loaf pan (6x3 inches) that fits in air fryer
  - Pour batter into pan
  - Preheat air fryer to 310째F (154째C)
  - Place loaf pan in air fryer basket
  - Cook for 25-30 minutes until toothpick comes out clean
  - If top browns too quickly, cover loosely with foil
  - Let cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing
  - Slice and serve

## Perfect Banana Bread Tips

- **Use very ripe bananas**: Black spots = best flavor
- **Don't overmix**: Mix until just combined
- **Check with toothpick**: Should come out with few crumbs
- **Cover if browning**: Prevents burnt top
- **Cool before slicing**: Makes cleaner slices

## Variations

**Chocolate Chip:**
- Add 1/2 cup chocolate chips

**Walnut:**
- Add 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

**Blueberry:**
- Fold in 1/2 cup fresh blueberries

**Cinnamon Swirl:**
- Swirl cinnamon sugar through batter

**Peanut Butter:**
- Add 1/4 cup peanut butter

**Nutella:**
- Swirl Nutella through batter

## Make It Healthier

- Replace butter with coconut oil
- Use whole wheat flour
- Reduce sugar to 1/3 cup
- Add flax seeds or chia seeds
- Use honey instead of sugar

## Storage

**Room Temperature:** 3 days wrapped in plastic
**Refrigerator:** 1 week in airtight container
**Freezer:** Up to 3 months, thaw at room temperature
