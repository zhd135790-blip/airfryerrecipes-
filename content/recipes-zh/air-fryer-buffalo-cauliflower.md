ingredients:
  - 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  - 1 cup all-purpose flour
  - 1 cup water
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  - 3/4 cup buffalo sauce (Frank's RedHot recommended)
  - 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegan butter
  - Cooking spray
  - Ranch or blue cheese dressing for serving
  - Celery sticks
instructions:
  - Cut cauliflower into bite-sized florets
  - In large bowl, whisk together flour, water, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper until smooth
  - Add cauliflower to batter and toss to coat completely
  - Preheat air fryer to 375°F (190°C)
  - Spray air fryer basket with cooking spray
  - Remove cauliflower from batter, letting excess drip off
  - Place in single layer in air fryer basket
  - Cook for 15 minutes, shaking basket halfway through
  - While cooking, mix buffalo sauce with melted butter
  - Remove cauliflower and place in large bowl
  - Toss with buffalo sauce mixture until well coated
  - Return to air fryer for 5 more minutes until crispy
  - Serve hot with ranch or blue cheese and celery sticks

## Perfect Buffalo Cauliflower Tips

- **Cut uniform**: Ensures even cooking
- **Thick batter**: Should coat florets well
- **First cook, then sauce**: Creates crispier texture
- **High heat finish**: Makes sauce sticky
- **Serve immediately**: Best when hot and crispy

## Spice Level Variations

**Mild:**
- Use 1/2 buffalo sauce + 1/2 BBQ sauce

**Medium:**
- Classic buffalo sauce ratio

**Hot:**
- Add extra hot sauce or cayenne

**Extra Hot:**
- Use extreme hot sauce brands

## Serving Ideas

**Game Day:**
- Serve on platter with multiple dipping sauces
- Add celery and carrot sticks
- Include blue cheese crumbles

**As Meal:**
- Serve over rice or quinoa
- Add to wraps or tacos
- Top salads

**Party Appetizer:**
- Toothpick for easy eating
- Variety of dipping sauces
- Keep warm in air fryer

## Dipping Sauce Options

- Classic ranch
- Blue cheese dressing
- Vegan ranch
- Garlic aioli
- Honey mustard
- Avocado lime crema

## Make It Vegan

- Use vegan butter
- Serve with vegan ranch
- Skip cheese dressing

## Storage

Store in refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in air fryer at 375°F for 5-7 minutes. Not recommended for freezing as cauliflower can become watery.
