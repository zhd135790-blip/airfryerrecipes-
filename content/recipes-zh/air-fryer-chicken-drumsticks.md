ingredients:
  - 8 chicken drumsticks
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 2 teaspoons paprika
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon onion powder
  - 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
instructions:
  - Pat chicken drumsticks dry with paper towels
  - Place in large bowl and drizzle with olive oil
  - In small bowl, mix paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, thyme, salt, pepper, and cayenne
  - Sprinkle seasoning mix over drumsticks and rub to coat evenly
  - Let sit for 10 minutes to marinate
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C)
  - Place drumsticks in air fryer basket, not overcrowding (may need to cook in batches)
  - Cook for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through
  - Check internal temperature reaches 175-180°F (79-82°C) for fall-off-bone tenderness
  - Let rest for 5 minutes before serving

## Perfect Drumsticks Tips

- **Pat very dry**: Key to crispy skin
- **Don't overcrowd**: Allows air circulation
- **Higher temp**: 400°F creates crispy skin
- **Flip once**: Ensures even browning
- **Use thermometer**: Guarantees perfect doneness

## Seasoning Variations

**BBQ:**
- Brush with BBQ sauce last 5 minutes

**Honey Garlic:**
- Honey, soy sauce, garlic glaze

**Lemon Herb:**
- Lemon zest, rosemary, thyme

**Buffalo:**
- Toss with buffalo sauce after cooking

**Teriyaki:**
- Marinate in teriyaki sauce

**Cajun:**
- Heavy Cajun seasoning

**Korean:**
- Gochujang, sesame, ginger

## Cooking Time by Size

**Small drumsticks:** 18-20 minutes
**Medium drumsticks:** 20-25 minutes
**Large drumsticks:** 25-28 minutes

Always check internal temperature!
