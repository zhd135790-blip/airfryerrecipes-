ingredients:
  - 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  - 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  - 1 cup cooked rice
  - 1 cup diced tomatoes
  - 1 small onion, diced
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  - 1 teaspoon cumin
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Fresh parsley for garnish
instructions:
  - Cut tops off bell peppers and remove seeds and membranes
  - In large skillet, cook ground beef until browned
  - Add onion and garlic, cook until softened
  - Stir in cooked rice, diced tomatoes, Italian seasoning, cumin, salt, and pepper
  - Cook for 5 minutes until flavors blend
  - Remove from heat and mix in 1/2 cup cheese
  - Stuff each pepper with meat and rice mixture
  - Preheat air fryer to 360°F (182°C)
  - Place stuffed peppers upright in air fryer basket
  - Cook for 15-18 minutes until peppers are tender
  - Top with remaining cheese
  - Cook for 2 more minutes until cheese melts
  - Garnish with fresh parsley and serve

## Perfect Stuffed Peppers Tips

- **Choose sturdy peppers**: Should stand upright
- **Pre-cook filling**: Ensures everything is cooked through
- **Don't overstuff**: Allows peppers to cook evenly
- **Check tenderness**: Peppers should be fork-tender
- **Add cheese last**: Prevents burning

## Filling Variations

**Mexican Style:**
- Ground beef, black beans, corn, taco seasoning
- Top with cheddar cheese and salsa

**Mediterranean:**
- Ground lamb, quinoa, feta, olives, sun-dried tomatoes

**Italian:**
- Italian sausage, marinara, mozzarella, basil

**Vegetarian:**
- Quinoa, black beans, corn, cheese

**Greek:**
- Ground beef, orzo, feta, spinach, tomatoes

## Grain Options

Instead of rice, try:
- Quinoa
- Cauliflower rice (low-carb)
- Couscous
- Orzo
- Brown rice
- Wild rice

## Make It Low-Carb

- Use cauliflower rice instead of regular rice
- Choose lean ground turkey
- Add extra vegetables

## Storage & Meal Prep

**Refrigerate:** Cooked stuffed peppers keep for 4 days
**Freeze:** Up to 3 months (best frozen before cooking)
**Reheat:** Air fryer at 350°F for 8-10 minutes
