## Perfect Corn Tips

- **Choose fresh corn**: Look for bright green husks
- **Turn regularly**: Ensures even cooking
- **Don't overcook**: Corn should be tender but not mushy
- **Butter while hot**: Absorbs flavor better
- **Season generously**: Corn can handle bold flavors

## Flavor Variations

**Mexican Street Corn (Elote):**
- Mayo, cotija cheese, chili powder, lime

**Garlic Parmesan:**
- Butter, garlic, grated Parmesan, parsley

**BBQ Butter:**
- Butter mixed with BBQ seasoning

**Cajun:**
- Cajun seasoning and butter

**Italian:**
- Olive oil, Italian herbs, Parmesan

**Asian-Inspired:**
- Sesame oil, soy sauce, sesame seeds

## Mexican Street Corn Recipe

After cooking, brush corn with:
1. Mayonnaise
2. Sprinkle with cotija cheese
3. Add chili powder and smoked paprika
4. Squeeze fresh lime juice
5. Garnish with cilantro

## Serving Suggestions

**Summer BBQ:**
- Perfect side for grilled meats
- Serve at cookouts
- Great for picnics

**Dinner Sides:**
- Pairs with chicken, steak, or fish
- Add to taco night
- Serve with burgers

## Storage

Store cooked corn in refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in air fryer at 350°F for 3-4 minutes. Can also cut kernels off cob for salads and other dishes.
