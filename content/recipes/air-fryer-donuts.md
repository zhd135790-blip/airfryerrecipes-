ingredients:
  - 2 cups all-purpose flour
  - 1/4 cup sugar
  - 1 teaspoon baking powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 cup milk
  - 2 tablespoons melted butter
  - 1 egg
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - Oil spray for coating
  - Powdered sugar or glaze for topping
instructions:
  - Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, salt) in a large bowl.
  - In another bowl, whisk together milk, melted butter, egg, and vanilla.
  - Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (don't overmix).
  - Turn dough onto floured surface and roll to 1/2 inch thickness.
  - Cut out donut shapes using a donut cutter or two different sized round cutters.
  - Spray air fryer basket with oil and place donuts in single layer.
  - Preheat air fryer to 350°F (175°C) and cook for 6-8 minutes.
  - Flip halfway through cooking.
  - Cool slightly before dusting with powdered sugar or glazing.

## Donut Variations

- **Cinnamon Sugar**: Toss warm donuts in cinnamon-sugar mixture
- **Chocolate Glaze**: Dip in melted chocolate and let set
- **Strawberry Glaze**: Mix powdered sugar with strawberry puree

## Pro Tips

- **Don't overmix**: Keep dough tender
- **Even thickness**: Ensures uniform cooking
- **Cool before glazing**: Prevents glaze from melting

## Storage

Best eaten fresh, but can be stored in an airtight container for 1-2 days.
