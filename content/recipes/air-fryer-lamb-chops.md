ingredients:
  - 8 lamb loin chops (1-inch thick)
  - 3 tablespoons olive oil
  - 4 cloves garlic, minced
  - 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  - 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  - Lemon wedges for serving
instructions:
  - Remove lamb chops from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking
  - Pat dry with paper towels
  - In small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, pepper, and Dijon mustard
  - Rub herb mixture generously on both sides of lamb chops
  - Let marinate for 15-20 minutes
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C)
  - Place lamb chops in air fryer basket in single layer
  - For medium-rare cook 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway (internal temp 130-135°F)
  - For medium cook 12-14 minutes (internal temp 140-145°F)
  - Remove and let rest for 5 minutes
  - Serve with lemon wedges

## Lamb Doneness Guide

**Rare:** 120-125°F (49-52°C) - 8-10 minutes
**Medium-Rare:** 130-135°F (54-57°C) - 10-12 minutes (recommended)
**Medium:** 140-145°F (60-63°C) - 12-14 minutes
**Medium-Well:** 150-155°F (66-68°C) - 14-16 minutes

## Perfect Lamb Chops Tips

- **Room temperature**: Critical for even cooking
- **Don't overcook**: Lamb is best at medium-rare
- **Fresh herbs**: Make huge flavor difference
- **Resting time**: Allows juices to redistribute
- **Use thermometer**: Only accurate way to check doneness
