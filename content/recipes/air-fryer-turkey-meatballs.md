ingredients:
  - 1.5 lbs ground turkey (93% lean)
  - 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  - 1/4 cup milk
  - 1 large egg
  - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  - 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  - Cooking spray
instructions:
  - In large bowl, combine breadcrumbs and milk, let soak for 5 minutes
  - Add ground turkey, Parmesan, egg, garlic, parsley, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and onion powder
  - Mix gently until just combined - don't overmix
  - Form into 18-20 meatballs, about 1.5 inches each
  - Preheat air fryer to 380°F (193°C)
  - Spray air fryer basket with cooking spray
  - Place meatballs in single layer, not touching
  - Spray tops lightly with cooking spray
  - Cook for 10-12 minutes, shaking basket halfway through
  - Check internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C)
  - Serve with marinara sauce and pasta, or in sandwiches

## Juicy Turkey Meatball Tips

- **Don't overmix**: Keeps meatballs tender
- **Milk-soaked breadcrumbs**: Adds moisture to lean turkey
- **Uniform size**: Ensures even cooking
- **Don't skip spray**: Helps browning
- **Let rest**: 5 minutes after cooking

## Flavor Variations

**Asian-Inspired:**
- Add ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil
- Serve with sweet chili sauce

**Greek:**
- Add feta cheese and oregano
- Serve with tzatziki

**Mexican:**
- Add cumin, chili powder, and cilantro
- Serve with salsa

**Teriyaki:**
- Glaze with teriyaki sauce in last 2 minutes

## Serving Ideas

**With Pasta:**
- Spaghetti and marinara
- Penne with vodka sauce
- Linguine with pesto

**As Sandwich:**
- Meatball sub with marinara and mozzarella
- Pita with tzatziki
- Slider buns with BBQ sauce

**In Bowls:**
- Over rice or quinoa
- With roasted vegetables
- Greek bowl with hummus

## Meal Prep

Make large batch and:
- **Refrigerate:** Up to 4 days
- **Freeze:** Up to 3 months
- **Reheat:** Air fryer at 350°F for 5-7 minutes
