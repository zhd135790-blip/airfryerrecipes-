ingredients:
  - 4 chicken breasts (6-8 oz each)
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  - 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
instructions:
  - Pat chicken breasts dry with paper towels and brush with olive oil
  - In a small bowl, mix garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper, thyme, and onion powder
  - Season both sides of chicken breasts with the spice mixture
  - Preheat air fryer to 375°F (190°C) for 3 minutes
  - Place chicken in air fryer basket, making sure they don't overlap
  - Cook for 10 minutes, then flip and cook for another 8-10 minutes
  - Check internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) with meat thermometer
  - Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing or serving

## Pro Tips for Perfect Chicken Breast

- **Pound evenly**: Use a meat mallet to ensure even thickness for consistent cooking
- **Don't overcook**: Remove when internal temperature reaches 165°F for juicy results
- **Let it rest**: Allow 5 minutes rest time for juices to redistribute

## Variations

- **Italian Style**: Add basil, oregano, and parmesan cheese
- **BBQ Style**: Brush with BBQ sauce in the last 5 minutes
- **Spicy**: Add cayenne pepper or chili powder to the seasoning mix

## Serving Suggestions

- Rice or quinoa
- Steamed vegetables
- Mashed potatoes
- Fresh salad

## Storage

Cooked chicken breast keeps in the fridge for up to 4 days. Reheat in air fryer at 350°F for 5-7 minutes.
