  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 tablespoon coarse salt
  - 1 teaspoon black pepper
  - Butter, sour cream, chives, bacon bits for topping
instructions:
  - Scrub potatoes clean and pat dry
  - Pierce each potato 5-6 times with fork
  - Rub potatoes with olive oil
  - Sprinkle with coarse salt and pepper
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C)
  - Place potatoes in air fryer basket, not touching
  - Cook for 35-40 minutes for medium potatoes
  - Flip potatoes halfway through cooking
  - Check doneness by inserting fork - should slide in easily
  - Remove and let rest for 5 minutes
  - Cut open with knife, fluff insides with fork
  - Add your favorite toppings and serve

## Perfect Baked Potato Tips

- **Choose right size**: Medium potatoes cook most evenly
- **Pierce before cooking**: Prevents explosions
- **Rub with oil**: Creates crispy skin
- **Coarse salt**: Makes skin extra crispy and flavorful
- **Don't skip flip**: Ensures even cooking

## Cooking Time Guide

**Small potatoes (6 oz):** 30-35 minutes
**Medium potatoes (8 oz):** 35-40 minutes
**Large potatoes (10 oz):** 40-45 minutes
**Extra large (12 oz):** 45-50 minutes
