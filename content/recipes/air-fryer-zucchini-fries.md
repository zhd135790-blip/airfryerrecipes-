ingredients:
  - 3 medium zucchini
  - 1/2 cup all-purpose flour (or almond flour for low-carb)
  - 2 eggs, beaten
  - 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  - 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Cooking spray
  - Ranch or marinara for dipping
instructions:
  - Cut zucchini into sticks about 3 inches long and 1/2 inch thick
  - Pat dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture
  - Set up breading station - flour, beaten eggs, and panko mixed with Parmesan and spices
  - Season flour with salt and pepper
  - Coat each zucchini stick in flour, then egg, then panko mixture
  - Press breadcrumbs firmly onto zucchini
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C)
  - Spray air fryer basket with cooking spray
  - Place zucchini fries in single layer, not touching
  - Spray tops generously with cooking spray
  - Cook for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden brown
  - Serve immediately with dipping sauce

## Tips for Crispy Zucchini Fries

- **Remove moisture**: Pat very dry with paper towels
- **Cut uniform**: Ensures even cooking
- **Press coating**: Helps breadcrumbs stick
- **Don't overcrowd**: Allows proper crisping
- **Serve immediately**: Best when hot and fresh

## Low-Carb Option

For keto/low-carb version:
- Use almond flour instead of all-purpose
- Replace panko with crushed pork rinds or almond flour
- Add extra Parmesan for flavor

## Storage & Reheating

Best eaten immediately, but can store leftovers for 1-2 days. Reheat in air fryer at 375°F for 5 minutes to restore some crispiness.
