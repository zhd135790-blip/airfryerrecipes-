## Tips for Perfect Wings

- **Dry is key**: Make sure wings are thoroughly patted dry before seasoning
- **Don't overcrowd**: Cook in batches if needed to ensure even crisping
- **Flip halfway**: This ensures even browning on all sides

## Sauce Suggestions

These wings are perfect with:
- Buffalo sauce
- BBQ sauce
- Honey garlic sauce
- Or enjoy them plain!

## Storage

Store leftover wings in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. Reheat in the air fryer at 350째F for 5-7 minutes.
