## Perfect Chicken Tenders Tips

- **Uniform size**: Ensures even cooking
- **Press breadcrumbs**: Creates better coating adhesion
- **Don't overcrowd**: Cook in batches if needed
- **Spray generously**: Key to golden, crispy exterior
- **Let rest**: 2-3 minutes before serving for maximum juiciness

## Dipping Sauce Ideas

**For Kids:**
- Honey mustard
- BBQ sauce
- Ranch dressing
- Ketchup

**For Adults:**
- Buffalo sauce
- Garlic aioli

## Make Your Own Sauces

- 1/4 cup Dijon mustard + 1/4 cup honey

**Buffalo:**
- Equal parts hot sauce and melted butter

**Ranch:**
- Buttermilk, mayo, herbs, garlic
