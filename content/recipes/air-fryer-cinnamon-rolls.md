ingredients:
  - 1 package (8 rolls) refrigerated cinnamon rolls with icing
  - Cooking spray
  - 4 oz cream cheese, 1/4 cup butter, 1 cup powdered sugar, 1 tsp vanilla (optional, for homemade frosting)
instructions:
  - Remove cinnamon rolls from package and separate
  - Cut parchment paper to fit air fryer basket
  - Spray parchment with cooking spray
  - Place cinnamon rolls on parchment, spacing 1 inch apart
  - Preheat air fryer to 320°F (160°C)
  - Place parchment with rolls in air fryer basket
  - Cook for 7-8 minutes until golden brown and cooked through
  - They should be puffed and no longer doughy
  - Remove and let cool for 2-3 minutes
  - Spread with included icing or homemade frosting
  - Serve warm

## Perfect Air Fryer Cinnamon Rolls Tips

- **Use parchment**: Prevents sticking
- **Space properly**: Allows for expanding
- **Low temperature**: Prevents burnt outsides
- **Check early**: Air fryers vary in temperature
- **Frost while warm**: Icing melts perfectly

## Homemade Frosting

Beat together:
- 4 oz cream cheese, softened
- 1/4 cup butter, softened
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

## Upgrade Ideas

**Extra Cinnamon Sugar:**
- Sprinkle more before cooking

**Pecans:**
- Add chopped pecans on top

**Caramel:**
- Drizzle with caramel sauce

**Apple Pie:**
- Add diced apples between layers

**Chocolate Chip:**
- Press chocolate chips into rolls

**Pumpkin Spice:**
- Add pumpkin pie spice to frosting

## Make-Ahead

Prepare rolls night before:
1. Place in air fryer basket
2. Cover and refrigerate overnight
3. Cook in morning, adding 2-3 minutes

## Serving Suggestions

**Breakfast:**
- Serve with coffee
- Add fresh fruit
- Include bacon or sausage

**Brunch:**
- Arrange on platter
- Serve with scrambled eggs
- Add orange juice

**Dessert:**
- Warm with ice cream
- Drizzle with extra icing
- Top with whipped cream

## Variations Using Crescent Dough

Make from scratch using crescent roll dough:
1. Roll out dough
2. Spread with butter and cinnamon sugar
3. Roll up and slice
4. Cook as directed

## Storage

**Room Temperature:** 2 days covered
**Refrigerator:** Up to 5 days
**Reheat:** Air fryer at 300°F for 2-3 minutes
