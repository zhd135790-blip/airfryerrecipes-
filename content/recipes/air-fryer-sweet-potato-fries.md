ingredients:
  - 2 large sweet potatoes (about 1.5 lbs)
  - 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
instructions:
  - Peel sweet potatoes and cut into 1/4-inch thick fries
  - Soak in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch
  - Drain and pat completely dry with paper towels
  - Place fries in large bowl and toss with cornstarch until coated
  - Add olive oil and toss to coat evenly
  - Mix all seasonings and sprinkle over fries, toss well

## Secrets to Crispy Sweet Potato Fries

- **Cornstarch magic**: Creates extra crispy exterior
- **Soak in water**: Removes excess starch
- **Cut uniform**: Ensures even cooking
- **Don't skip drying**: Moisture prevents crisping
- **Single layer**: Space between fries is crucial
