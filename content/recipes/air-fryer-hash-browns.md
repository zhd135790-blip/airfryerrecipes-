## Secrets to Crispy Hash Browns

- **Remove ALL moisture**: This is the #1 key to crispiness
- **Use russet potatoes**: Best starch content for crispy results
- **Press firmly**: Creates better contact with basket
- **Don't flip too early**: Let crust form first
- **High heat finish**: Creates extra golden crust
