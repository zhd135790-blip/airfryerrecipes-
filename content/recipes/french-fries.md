## The Secret to Crispy Fries

1. **Soak the potatoes**: This removes excess starch
2. **Dry thoroughly**: Moisture is the enemy of crispiness
3. **Don't overcrowd**: Give fries space to crisp up
4. **Shake regularly**: Ensures even cooking
