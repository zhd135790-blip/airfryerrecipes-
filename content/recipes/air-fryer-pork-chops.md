ingredients:
  - 4 bone-in pork chops (1-inch thick)
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon onion powder
  - 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/2 teaspoon brown sugar
instructions:
  - Pat pork chops dry with paper towels
  - Brush both sides with olive oil
  - Mix all seasonings in a small bowl
  - Rub seasoning mixture generously on both sides of pork chops
  - Let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C)
  - Place pork chops in air fryer basket in single layer
  - Cook for 12-15 minutes, flipping halfway through
  - Check internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C)
  - Let rest for 5 minutes before serving

## Pro Cooking Tips

- **Thickness matters**: Adjust cooking time based on pork chop thickness (add 2-3 minutes for extra thick)
- **Don't overcook**: Remove at 145°F for juicy results
- **Resting time**: Always let rest for maximum juiciness
- **Pat dry**: Ensures better browning and crispiness

## Flavor Variations

- **Garlic Herb**: Add fresh rosemary and extra garlic
- **Cajun**: Use Cajun seasoning for spicy kick
- **Honey Mustard**: Brush with honey mustard glaze in last 2 minutes
- **Apple Glazed**: Top with sautéed apples and onions
