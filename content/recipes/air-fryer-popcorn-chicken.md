ingredients:
  - 1.5 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch cubes
  - 1 cup all-purpose flour
  - 2 eggs, beaten
  - 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon onion powder
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - Cooking spray
instructions:
  - Cut chicken into 1-inch bite-sized pieces
  - Pat dry with paper towels
  - Set up breading station with three bowls - flour, beaten eggs, and panko mixed with Parmesan and spices
  - Season flour with salt and pepper
  - Working in batches, coat chicken pieces in flour, then egg, then panko mixture
  - Press breadcrumbs firmly onto chicken
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C)
  - Spray air fryer basket with cooking spray
  - Place chicken pieces in single layer, not touching
  - Spray tops generously with cooking spray
  - Cook for 10-12 minutes, shaking basket halfway through
  - Check internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C)
  - Serve hot with favorite dipping sauces

## Perfect Popcorn Chicken Tips

- **Uniform size**: Ensures even cooking
- **Don't skip spray**: Creates golden, crispy exterior
- **Single layer**: Cook in batches if needed
- **Shake basket**: Promotes even browning
- **Serve immediately**: Best when hot and crispy

## Flavor Variations

**Spicy:**
- Add cayenne pepper to breading

**BBQ:**
- Mix BBQ seasoning into breadcrumbs

**Ranch:**
- Add ranch seasoning powder

**Buffalo:**
- Toss with buffalo sauce after cooking

**Honey Mustard:**
- Serve with honey mustard for dipping

## Storage & Reheating

**Refrigerate:** Up to 3 days in airtight container
**Freeze:** Up to 2 months
**Reheat:** Air fryer at 375°F for 5-7 minutes until crispy

## Make-Ahead

Bread chicken pieces and freeze on baking sheet. Once frozen, transfer to freezer bags. Cook from frozen, adding 3-5 minutes to cooking time.
