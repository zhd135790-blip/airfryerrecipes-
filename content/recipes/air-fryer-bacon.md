## Pro Tips for Perfect Bacon

- **Don't overlap**: Single layer ensures even cooking
- **Check at 8 minutes**: Cooking time varies by thickness
- **Paper towels**: Essential for removing excess grease

## Variations

- **Peppered Bacon**: Add fresh cracked pepper before cooking
- **Maple Glazed**: Brush with maple syrup in the last 2 minutes
- **Candied**: Sprinkle with brown sugar before cooking

## Storage

Cooked bacon keeps in the fridge for up to 4 days. Reheat in air fryer at 350°F for 2-3 minutes.
