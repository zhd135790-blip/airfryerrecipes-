ingredients:
  - 1 block firm tofu (14 oz)
  - 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  - Salt and pepper to taste
instructions:
  - Press tofu for 15-20 minutes to remove excess moisture.
  - Cut tofu into 1-inch cubes and pat completely dry.
  - In a bowl, toss tofu with cornstarch until evenly coated.
  - Add olive oil, soy sauce, and seasonings. Toss to combine.
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (200°C).
  - Place tofu in single layer in air fryer basket.
  - Cook for 12-15 minutes, shaking basket every 5 minutes.
  - Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce or over rice.

## Tofu Pressing Tips

- **Use a tofu press** or wrap in paper towels with heavy books on top
- **Press for 15-20 minutes** for best results
- **Pat completely dry** before seasoning

## Serving Ideas

- **Buddha Bowl**: Over quinoa with vegetables
- **Stir Fry**: Add to your favorite stir fry
- **Tacos**: Use in place of meat
- **Salad Topper**: Add to green salads

## Flavor Variations

- **Teriyaki**: Use teriyaki sauce instead of soy sauce
- **Buffalo**: Toss with buffalo sauce after cooking
- **BBQ**: Use BBQ seasoning and sauce
