ingredients:
  - 2 cups broccoli florets
  - 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  - 1 yellow bell pepper, cut into chunks
  - 1 zucchini, sliced
  - 1 red onion, cut into wedges
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  - 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)
instructions:
  - Wash and cut all vegetables into similar-sized pieces for even cooking.
  - In a large bowl, toss vegetables with olive oil and seasonings.
  - Preheat air fryer to 375°F (190°C).
  - Add vegetables to air fryer basket in a single layer (cook in batches if needed).
  - Cook for 10 minutes, shake the basket, then cook for another 8-10 minutes.
  - Vegetables should be tender and slightly charred on the edges.
  - Serve immediately, optionally topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

## Why Air Fry Vegetables?

Air frying vegetables gives you that perfect roasted texture with:
- Less oil than traditional roasting
- Faster cooking time
- Easy cleanup
- Consistent results every time

## Customization Ideas

Feel free to use any vegetables you like:
- Brussels sprouts
- Cauliflower
- Carrots
- Asparagus
- Sweet potatoes

## Meal Prep Friendly

These vegetables are perfect for meal prep! Store in airtight containers for up to 4 days and reheat in the air fryer for 3-4 minutes.
