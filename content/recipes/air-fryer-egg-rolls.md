ingredients:
  - 12 egg roll wrappers
  - 1 lb ground pork
  - 2 cups shredded cabbage
  - 1 cup shredded carrots
  - 4 green onions, chopped
  - 3 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  - 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  - 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  - 1 teaspoon sugar
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - Cooking spray
  - Sweet and sour sauce for dipping
instructions:
  - In large skillet over medium-high heat, cook ground pork until browned
  - Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute
  - Add cabbage, carrots, and green onions, cook until softened
  - Stir in soy sauce, sesame oil, and sugar
  - Season with salt and pepper, then cool completely
  - Place egg roll wrapper with corner pointing toward you
  - Add 3 tablespoons filling in center
  - Fold bottom corner over filling, then fold in sides
  - Roll tightly, sealing top corner with water
  - Repeat with remaining wrappers and filling
  - Preheat air fryer to 390°F (199°C)
  - Spray egg rolls generously with cooking spray
  - Place in air fryer basket seam-side down, not touching
  - Cook for 10-12 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden brown
  - Serve hot with sweet and sour sauce

## Tips for Perfect Egg Rolls

- **Cool filling completely**: Prevents soggy wrappers
- **Roll tightly**: Keeps filling from escaping
- **Seal well**: Use water to seal edges
- **Spray generously**: Essential for golden, crispy texture
- **Seam side down**: Prevents unwrapping during cooking

## Filling Variations

**Vegetarian:**
- Replace pork with mushrooms and tofu

**Shrimp:**
- Use chopped shrimp instead of pork

**Chicken:**
- Ground chicken or diced chicken breast

**Southwestern:**
- Black beans, corn, peppers, and cheese

## Make-Ahead & Freezing

**To Freeze:**
1. Assemble egg rolls but don't cook
2. Place on baking sheet and freeze until solid
3. Transfer to freezer bags, store up to 3 months
4. Cook from frozen, adding 5 extra minutes

## Dipping Sauce Ideas

- Sweet and sour sauce (classic)
- Duck sauce
- Hot mustard
- Soy ginger dipping sauce
- Sweet chili sauce
- Plum sauce

## Party Serving Tips

- Cut diagonally for presentation
- Serve with variety of dipping sauces
- Keep warm in air fryer at low temperature
- Make day ahead and reheat before serving
