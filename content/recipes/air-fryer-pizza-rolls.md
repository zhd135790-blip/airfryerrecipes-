ingredients:
  - 24 egg roll wrappers
  - 1 cup pizza sauce
  - 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  - 1/2 cup mini pepperoni (or regular pepperoni, chopped)
  - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - Cooking spray
  - Extra pizza sauce for dipping
instructions:
  - Mix mozzarella, Parmesan, Italian seasoning, and garlic powder in bowl
  - Place egg roll wrapper on clean surface, corner facing you
  - Spread 1 teaspoon pizza sauce in center
  - Add 1 tablespoon cheese mixture and 3-4 pepperoni pieces
  - Fold bottom corner over filling, fold in sides, roll tightly
  - Seal top corner with water
  - Repeat with remaining wrappers
  - Preheat air fryer to 380°F (193°C)
  - Spray air fryer basket with cooking spray
  - Place pizza rolls seam-side down in single layer
  - Spray tops with cooking spray
  - Cook for 6-8 minutes until golden and crispy
  - Let cool for 2-3 minutes before eating (filling is hot!)
  - Serve with warm pizza sauce for dipping

## Perfect Pizza Roll Tips

- **Don't overfill**: Prevents bursting during cooking
- **Seal well**: Use water to seal edges
- **Cool before eating**: Filling gets extremely hot
- **Single layer**: Don't stack or overlap
- **Watch closely**: They can brown quickly

## Filling Variations

**Classic Pepperoni:**
- Mozzarella, pepperoni, pizza sauce

**Supreme:**
- Add sausage, peppers, onions

**Hawaiian:**
- Ham, pineapple, mozzarella

**Veggie:**
- Mushrooms, peppers, onions, olives

**BBQ Chicken:**
- Chicken, BBQ sauce, red onion, cheddar

**Buffalo Chicken:**
- Chicken, buffalo sauce, ranch, mozzarella

## Make-Ahead & Freezing

**To Freeze:**
1. Assemble pizza rolls but don't cook
2. Place on baking sheet and freeze until solid
3. Transfer to freezer bags
4. Store up to 3 months
5. Cook from frozen, adding 2-3 minutes
