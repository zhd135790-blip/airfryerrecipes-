ingredients:
  - 4 tilapia fillets (6 oz each)
  - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  - Lemon wedges for serving
  - Fresh parsley for garnish
instructions:
  - Pat tilapia fillets dry with paper towels
  - Brush both sides with olive oil
  - In small bowl, mix paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, dried parsley, salt, pepper, and cayenne
  - Season both sides of fillets with spice mixture
  - Preheat air fryer to 400°F (204°C)
  - Place tilapia fillets in air fryer basket in single layer
  - Cook for 8-10 minutes until fish flakes easily with fork
  - Internal temperature should reach 145°F (63°C)
  - Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges

## Perfect Tilapia Tips

- **Don't overcook**: Fish should be just opaque and flaky
- **Pat dry**: Removes excess moisture for better seasoning
- **Check thickness**: Adjust time for thicker/thinner fillets
- **Single layer**: Ensures even cooking
- **Fresh is best**: Use freshest fish available
