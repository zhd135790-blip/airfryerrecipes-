ingredients:
  - 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  - 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  - 1 large egg
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 2 tablespoons sour cream
  - 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  - 6 vanilla wafer cookies (or graham cracker crust)
  - Fresh berries, whipped cream, fruit sauce (for toppings)
instructions:
  - Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth and fluffy
  - Add egg, vanilla extract, sour cream, and lemon juice
  - Mix until well combined and smooth
  - Place silicone cupcake liners in ramekins or oven-safe cups
  - Put one vanilla wafer in bottom of each liner as crust
  - Divide cheesecake batter evenly among cups
  - Preheat air fryer to 300°F (150°C)
  - Place ramekins in air fryer basket, don't stack
  - Cook for 18-20 minutes until edges are set but center still jiggles slightly
  - Turn off air fryer and let cheesecakes sit inside for 10 minutes
  - Remove and cool to room temperature
  - Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving
  - Top with berries, whipped cream, or fruit sauce

## Perfect Cheesecake Tips

- **Room temperature cream cheese**: Essential for smooth texture
- **Don't overmix**: Can incorporate too much air
- **Low temperature**: Prevents cracking
- **Jiggle test**: Center should wobble when done
- **Chill completely**: Allows cheesecake to set properly

## Flavor Variations

**Chocolate:**
- Add 1/4 cup melted chocolate

**Strawberry:**
- Swirl in strawberry puree

**Lemon:**
- Add extra lemon zest and juice

**Pumpkin:**
- Add pumpkin puree and spices

**Oreo:**
- Mix in crushed Oreos

**Salted Caramel:**
- Drizzle with caramel sauce

**Fruit:**

- Cherry pie filling
- Lemon curd

**Decadent:**
- Chocolate ganache
- Caramel sauce

## Storage

Refrigerate covered for up to 5 days. Can also freeze for up to 2 months. Thaw in refrigerator overnight before serving.

## Make-Ahead

Perfect for parties! Make 2-3 days ahead and store covered in refrigerator. Add toppings just before serving.
