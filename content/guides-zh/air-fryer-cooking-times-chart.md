## Pro Tips for Perfect Results

### General Guidelines

1. **Preheat**: Always preheat air fryer for 3-5 minutes
2. **Don't Overcrowd**: Leave space for air circulation
3. **Oil Spray**: Light spray helps with browning
4. **Shake/Flip**: Most foods benefit from flipping halfway
5. **Check Early**: Cooking times vary by model

## Safety Tips

- **Internal Temperature**: Always use meat thermometer
- **Even Cooking**: Shake basket or flip food halfway through
- **Hot Surfaces**: Use oven mitts - basket gets very hot
- **Steam Release**: Be careful when opening - hot steam escapes
- **Clean Regularly**: Prevents smoke and maintains performance

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| **Food not crispy** | Use more oil spray, increase temperature, or cook longer |
| **Uneven cooking** | Don't overcrowd, shake basket more often |
| **Smoking** | Clean basket, reduce oil amount |
| **Food sticking** | Use parchment paper or more oil spray |
| **Too dry** | Reduce cooking time, use marinade |
| **Burnt outside, raw inside** | Lower temperature, cook longer |

## Altitude Adjustments

If you're at high altitude (above 3,000 feet):
- **Add 5-10 minutes** to cooking time
- **Monitor closely** - results may vary
- **May need slightly higher temperature**
